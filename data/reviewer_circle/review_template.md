## How to score each dimension

- **Rigor:** Is the scientific reasoning sound? Are claims proportionate to the evidence presented? Are methodological choices justified?
- **Novelty:** Does the output make a non-obvious contribution? Would an expert in the area find it interesting or surprising?
- **Grounding:** Are claims supported by appropriate citations or data? Are mechanisms invoked correctly attributed to primary literature?
- **Clarity:** Is the output clearly written and logically organized? Could a colleague in the field follow the argument?
- **Feasibility:** For hypotheses and proposals: could this be tested with currently available methods and data? For analyses: does the approach seem executable?
