# Tealc

Personal AI postdoc for the Blackmon Lab at Texas A&M.

**Connected tools**

`gmail` · `calendar` · `drive` · `docs` · `sheets` · `pubmed` · `openalex` · `biorxiv`

**File attachments**

Drop a PDF or DOCX into the chat and Tealc will read it.

**Models**

Default: Sonnet 4.6. Say **"think hard"** or **"use opus"** to switch to Opus 4.7 for deep scientific work.

**Background loop**

Tealc runs overnight — triaging email, scanning preprints, flagging deadlines. Pending briefings surface at the top of each new session.

**Memory**

Conversation memory persists across sessions and machines via Google Drive.

**Ask Tealc directly**

- *"What can you do for me?"* — full programmatic summary of every tool and scheduled job, grouped by category + cadence.
- *"Run <job_name> now"* — force-runs any background job immediately, bypassing its working-hours guard (e.g. *"run wiki_janitor now"*, *"trigger paper_of_the_day"*).
